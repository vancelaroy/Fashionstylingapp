Guidelines
Everything you need to know to participate. Enter by Jun 18, 11:59pm PDT.

Welcome to the Config Makeathon!
This is your chance to build that one idea that solves that problem you’ve been thinking about, with a chance to win a share of $100k.

‼️ The Challenge
Design matters now more than ever. This is your chance to design a solution to a problem, to test out new tools and use them to design and build something that makes a difference. We want to see how you use Figma to build something with purpose and meaning, something that improves your life, your community, or the world.
📋 Submission Guidelines
Must include a video walking through the work and the idea behind it, sharing the problem you are solving with your build, and the workflow used to bring the idea to life.
Must be made using Figma’s suite of products (Figma Make, MCP, agent, etc.) See the Tools section below for more info.
Must include link(s) to your project (live project link and community or working project file link)
Must share your video to social! Check out the last Makeathon’s winners here.
✚ Bonus:
Sharing to the Figma community receives bonus points and could be featured at the top of the figma.com/community site!
🧮 Judging Criteria
The winning submissions will be selected based on one or more of the following criteria:
Quality of work across design, build, impact, and craft.
Quality of the idea and its ability to solve a real problem for yourself, your community or the world.
Quality of the video showcasing your workflow, the design decisions behind the project, and steps others could take to emulate their process.
Building in Figma in a novel, innovative or unexpected way across Make, MCP, Local, Weave, design agent, etc.
Scoring:
5 points per judging category
5 points for projects shared on social
5 points for Figma projects share to the Figma Community
Figma Product Access
Figma Pro:
All participants get access to Figma Pro for the duration of the Config Makeathon, including 3k AI credits. After joining, check your confirmation email from Contra and click the access code link to upgrade a free Starter team to a Professional team for free. 
Figma Weave:
All participants get access to sign up for a new Figma Weave account including 1.5k AI credits. 
Agent Beta: 
The first 10k people who preregister before June 3 at 5pm PST will get access to the Beta of Figma design agent*. 
After joining, check your confirmation email from Contra and click the access code link to upgrade a free Starter team to a Professional team and to sign up for a Figma Weave account.
*Note: Agent beta access is not guaranteed for the duration of the event and may be revoked without notice
